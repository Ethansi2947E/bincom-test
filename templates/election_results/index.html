About This Portal

This Election Results Portal provides access to election results data at both the polling unit and local government area levels. You can view detailed results for individual polling units, see aggregated results for entire local government areas, and store new results as they become available.

The system is designed to ensure transparency and accessibility of election data for all stakeholders.
